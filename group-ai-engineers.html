Who We Are

Our Position

We are AI Engineers — the people who actually build AI systems. Companies, model providers, and data centres all want to hire us. We care about fair working conditions, ethical limits on what we build, and the futures of workers whose jobs AI is changing. We have leverage in this debate: no one can do this without us.

We represent the technical workforce powering the AI revolution. Our role goes beyond writing code — we make the daily decisions that shape whether AI systems are safe, efficient, and accountable. When a model is deployed recklessly, we bear some of that responsibility. When AI displaces workers, we feel it too.

Europe needs to retain AI talent. Brain drain to the US is real and costly. We support open-source AI development which reduces the concentration of power and enables broader scrutiny. We also support retraining funds for displaced workers — this is not just social responsibility, it is the right and fair thing to do.

"We are not just builders. We are the conscience of the AI industry. No one can build this without us — which means we have both the power and the responsibility to get it right."

Our Strongest Arguments
  • Europe needs to retain AI talent — brain drain to the US is real and costly
  • Engineers have ethical agency: we can refuse to build harmful systems
  • Without us, no AI model gets built — we have leverage in every negotiation
  • Open-source AI development reduces concentration of power
  • We support retraining funds for displaced workers
