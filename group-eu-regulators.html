Who We Are

Our Position

We are the EU Regulators. Our role in this manifesto is not to pick winners or to slow progress down — it is to hold the balance. AI is one of the most powerful tools of our generation, and Europe should build it, lead in it, and benefit from it. But growth that costs us our environment, our rights, or the trust of the people it serves is not progress at all.

So we sit between the groups — providers, data centres, engineers, businesses, and environmentalists — and we listen to all of them. Our job is to keep innovation and competitiveness alive while making sure AI is launched sustainably and responsibly. We have the final word in a dispute, but only ever in service of a fair outcome that every group can stand behind.

We prepared version 0.1 and version 0.2 of this manifesto, incorporating every sector's voice. We have drafted the text, tracked the open points, and held this process together. The manifesto you are reading is ours — and equally, it belongs to every group that signed it.

"Balancing innovation with responsibility — so AI leaves the next generation better off."

Growth
