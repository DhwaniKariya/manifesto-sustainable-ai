Who We Are

Our Position

We represent companies using AI across every major industry. As the primary buyers and deployers of AI systems, we have significant leverage over how AI is built and governed. Our procurement decisions and sustainability requirements can drive industry-wide change.

We are not passive consumers of AI — we are responsible deployers. When we buy an AI system, we take on accountability for how it is used. We believe in human oversight for high-stakes decisions, in transparency with the people AI affects, and in ensuring that when AI changes a job, the worker affected is supported.

We want clear, predictable rules. The EU AI Act's risk-tier framework gives us a structure to work with. We are committed to applying it faithfully — not just to comply, but because responsible AI adoption protects our reputation, our workforce, and our customers.
